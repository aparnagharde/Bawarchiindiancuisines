Our Concept

Bawarchi Indian Cuisine

A wide range of aromatic spices come together to create a beautiful pot of Biryani. Biryani is not only popular, but also one of the most loved delicacies in the culinary world . For anyone craving for authentic Dum Biryani, Bawarchi is your place to go. We promise to make your dining experience memorable. Our aim is to create a sense of home away from home for all the food lovers in America. Stop by and dine in at your nearest Bawarchi restaurant to enjoy our food and hospitality.

Bawarchi Biryanis

Bawarchi Biryanis is our full service restaurant concept and is home to an elaborate menu of Biryanis, and an extensive array of Pan Indian dishes. Our signature dish, Hyderabadi Dum Biryani stands as a benchmark for Biryanis in America and is the most ordered dish among the Biryani aficionados. We are known for our signature curries like Butter Chicken, Gongura Mutton, Shahi Paneer. Our weekend specials like Natukodi Fry, Andhra Mutton Korma, Royyala Vepudu and Bawarchi Tiffins like Masala Dosa, Rava Dosa, Idli/Vada and Pesarattu are our customer favorites. We also offer catering services for all occasions from Weddings to Birthday Parties. Make Bawarchi Biryanis your favorite place to dine.

Bawarchi Express

Bawarchi Express is the Quick Service takeout concept of Bawarchi group. This concept has been specially designed for our customers, who want to enjoy a quick delicious meal at the comfort of their home or office. Menu is focused on quick service items including Biryanis, Popular Curries and Appetizers. Had a busy day at work and want to relax with a plateful of biryani at home or want to host a gathering with tasty starters and finger licking curries, Bawarchi Express is your place “TO GO”

Food Truck

Bawarchi Biryanis & Bakery (HotBreads) is the place to go for mouth watering Indian bakery items along with mouth watering Indian Street Food. This concept offers a very fine quality of Indian Style Pastries, Cakes, Cookies and Breads. Eggless Cakes, Pastries and Breads are a unique offering for Vegetarians. This concept also offers wide ranging popular street foods including Pav Bhaji, Vada Pav, Pizzas, Burgers/Sandwiches, Biryanis and quick service meals

About Bawarchi Restaurants

About Bawarchi

India is a land of rich history and diverse cultures. Indian food reflects a perfect blend of this diversity and cultural influences. We at Bawarchis create delicious Indian food with authentic flavor, offering you the best of South and North Indian cuisine, a cuisine that has inspired the Culinary world.

Bawarchi Biryanis isn’t just about food, it is about our family and traditions.

The food you love...The Tradition we follow... We welcome you to indulge in our traditional creations.... We serve a wide variety of authentic Hyderabadi, Indian and Indo Chinese cuisine. Cooking is an art and our chefs have mastered the art by combining traditional recipes and adding their own personal touch to it. Every delicious dish that leaves our kitchen is created by one of our highly acclaimed chefs. Whether you eat a simple Chicken tikka kebab or our signature dish Hyderabad Nawabi Chicken Dum Biryani, you are sure to enjoy the tantalizing flavors we have to offer.

FOOD HERE IS NOT JUST GOOD,IT IS FINGER LICKING GOOD

Our Mission

Our vision is to make Bawarchi brand, the national ambassador for Indian cuisine. We aspire to spread the taste of our gourmet menu in every multi cultural city of the United States. With this vision, we celebrate the taste of Indian food with four concepts, Bawarchi Biryanis, Bawarchi Biryanis and Bakery, Bawarchi Express, and Bawarchi Swagruha.

Our Core Values

Bawarchi Group is not just a group of restaurants dedicated to quality food and exemplary service, but a group of individuals committed to the common goal of making fond memories for our guestsand elevating the standards and expectations of restaurant industry. We at Bawarchi, do not just say our core values, but we live by them.

Honesty and Respect : We act ethically and honorably in every aspect and respect one another regardless of race, gender, religion, social status, or any other distinction..

Integrity At Bawarchi Group, we conduct all matters of business with integrity. We proudly uphold the values of honesty, truthfulness and sincerity.

Our Vision

Our mission is to create a memorable experience filled with happy moments and wonderful flavors. While the fusion of fresh ingredients and bold flavors are sure to tickle your taste buds, the atmosphere rich in Indian style and hospitality welcomes every guest with a warmth that is truly Indian.
