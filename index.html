About Bawarchi Restaurants

A visionary behind the Bawarchi Indian Cuisine brand Success - with two decades of management and operations experience, Kishore Kancharla seamlessly transitioned from the IT sector to establish Bawarchi Indian Cuisine as a global restaurant chain.

Journey that amalgamated the culture, tradition, and taste - As we reflect on these remarkable 13 years, we extend our heartfelt gratitude to our patrons for their loyalty and discerning taste, which have been the driving force behind our journey. The Bawarchi Indian Cuisine experience is not just about savoring exquisite dishes; it is an immersive celebration of culture, tradition, and the vibrant tapestry of Indian cuisine.

Expanding Horizons, Innovating Traditions - As Bawarchi Indian Cuisine commemorates its 13th anniversary, the brand looks toward the future with a steadfast commitment to its mission of spreading flavors and enchanting patrons with the essence of authentic Indian cuisine. The journey ahead involves not only further expansion but also a dedication to innovation that keeps pace with evolving food industry trends.

Our Concept

Bawarchi Biryanis is our full service restaurant concept and is home to an elaborate menu of Biryanis, and an extensive array of Pan Indian dishes. Our signature dish, Hyderabadi Dum Biryani stands as a benchmark for Biryanis in America and is the most ordered dish among the Biryani aficionados. We are known for our signature curries like Butter Chicken, Gongura Mutton, Shahi Paneer. Our weekend specials like Natukodi Fry, Andhra Mutton Korma, Royyala Vepudu and Bawarchi Tiffins like Masala Dosa, Rava Dosa, Idli/Vada and Pesarattu are our customer favorites. We also offer catering services for all occasions from Weddings to Birthday Parties. Make Bawarchi Biryanis your favorite place to dine.

A wide range of aromatic spices come together to create a beautiful pot of Biryani. Biryani is not only popular, but also one of the most loved delicacies in the culinary world . For anyone craving for authentic Dum Biryani, Bawarchi is your place to go. We promise to make your dining experience memorable. Our aim is to create a sense of home away from home for all the food lovers in America. Stop by and dine in at your nearest Bawarchi restaurant to enjoy our food and hospitality.

Bawarchi Express is the Quick Service takeout concept of Bawarchi group. This concept has been specially designed for our customers, who want to enjoy a quick delicious meal at the comfort of their home or office. Menu is focused on quick service items including Biryanis, Popular Curries and Appetizers. Had a busy day at work and want to relax with a plateful of biryani at home or want to host a gathering with tasty starters and finger licking curries, Bawarchi Express is your place “TO GO”.

Indulge in the irresistible flavors of authentic Indian cuisine with a modern twist. At Bawarchi Biryanis, we're passionate about bringing you the freshest ingredients and the most tantalizing spices to create dishes that will delight your taste buds and transport you to the vibrant streets of India.

Bawarchi Biryanis & Bakery (HotBreads) is the place to go for mouth watering Indian bakery items along with mouth watering Indian Street Food. This concept offers a very fine quality of Indian Style Pastries, Cakes, Cookies and Breads. Eggless Cakes, Pastries and Breads are a unique offering for Vegetarians. This concept also offers wide ranging popular street foods including Pav Bhaji, Vada Pav, Pizzas, Burgers/Sandwiches, Biryanis and quick service meals.

Embark on a culinary journey like no other as we blend traditional Indian flavors with modern twists at Bawarchi Biryanis Bar and Grill. Situated in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers an inviting ambiance, attentive service, and a menu that promises to tantalize your taste buds.
